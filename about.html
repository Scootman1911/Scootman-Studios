Bio

Scootman Studios as an official entitiy began life on October 2, 2014. As an idea, however, Scootman Studios has been around since late 2004. What began as making simple drums beats for guitar backing tracks has evolved into a large variety of styles. Scootman Studios has been established to house the more Reggae and Hip-Hop styles of music whereas Eschaton Records has been established for the Metal and Rock styles.
